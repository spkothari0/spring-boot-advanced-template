## How to setup environment variables in IntellIj: goto run>Edit Configurations>Modify Options. Browse your env file there.

SPRING_APP_JWTSECRET=
SPRING_APP_JWTEXPIRATIONMS=

CACHE_ENABLED=false

REDIS_API_USER_KEY=
